IMAG

The International Moving Astronomy Gallery

A fictional organisation based on astronomical wonders.

Its main area of focus is unreal creations of celestial phenomena.

NASA's shuttle Endeavour and ISS as seen from a nearby Soyuz.

About IMAG

Fictional astronomical creations deployed into data files.


Founded in 2021 as a beginner ‘dashboard’ project, enhanced in 2024 as an non-official organisation.


Maintained by Atlas.

The Origin

of IMAG

It started with a Python code for the imaginary directory of IMAG.


The code included:

  1. About
  2. Activity Schedules
  3. Docent Guides
  4. Buggy Services


After that, there was nothing but emptiness.

February 2nd, 2024, marks the birth of IMAG’s first creation, Planet A3750-9.
